In addition to products, ICEE Solutions LLC provides short term services and support to accommodate various design styles and requests or meet tight design schedules.

CRATUS

CRATUS is a static low power verification and validation tool. It could checks 20+ various design problems, from power leakages to basic circuit integrity, which could accept Spice/Spectre/Verilog netlists of either the blocks level or SOC top level.

METIS

METIS is a semi-static, accurate function analysis/simulation tool for power verification and validation.
